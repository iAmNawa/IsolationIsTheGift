Isolation is the Gift...
If you are going to try, go all the way...
Otherwise, don't even start.

The poem "All the way" by Charles Bukowski is a poem that talks about going "all the way." He talks about isolation as being the gift. To me, this means that you must search within your inner self to find your purpose and meaning. This can only be accomplished by spending time in isolation...
